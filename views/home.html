Dan Cuellar

International Man of Quality

Currently known to be managing testing for Zoosk in San Francisco, Dan is a well-trained and very dangerous manager of quality assurance practices. In 2011, he created the mobile automation framework known as Appium. In the past, he has worked as an operative for Microsoft on products such as Office.com, Outlook for Mac and Excel. He has been known to show up to speak at conferences related to quality assurance and test automation.

Projects

Appium is a cross-platform mobile automation framework for writing user interface automation for iOS and Android applications. Dan started Appium back in 2011 as a means to mitigate the risks of releasing an iOS application. Due to the frequently lengthy AppStore review process, mistakes in an iOS application can have long-lived consequences for your business.
